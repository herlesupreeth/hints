
/dev/console

  The kernel console was configured by "console=" boot option.
  This option could be set multiple times for multiple consoles.

  printk() output messages to the console device.


/dev/tty0

  tty[1-63] are virtual terminals.

  Press Ctrl+Alt+Fn to switch to tty[n].


/dev/ttyS0

  This is the serial port console, and equals to tty0.
